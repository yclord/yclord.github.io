                                                  覆水难收

                                                  HaiyueAbout 1 min
                                                  ×

                                                  覆fù水shuǐ难nán收shōu

                                                  释义： 倒dǎo在zài地dì上shàng的de水shuǐ难nán收shōu回huí来lái。 比bǐ喻yù事shì情qíng已yǐ成chéng定dìng局jú，无wú法fǎ挽wǎn回huí。（多duō用yòng在zài夫fū妻qī离lí异yì）

                                                  用法： 当dāng年nián是shì你nǐ抛pāo弃qì他tā们men母mǔ子zi， 现xiàn在zài要yāo求qiú复fù婚hūn，恐kǒng怕pà是shì覆fù水shuǐ难nán收shōu了le。

                                                  What is done cannot be undone

                                                  How to use the metaphor: I’m afraid the harm you caused, when you abandoned the mother and child years ago, cannot be undone, even if you’re asking for reconciliation now.
