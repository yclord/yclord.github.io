                                                  光明正大

                                                  HaiyueAbout 1 min

                                                  光guāng明míng正zhèng大dà

                                                  释义： 形xíng容róng心xīn地dì纯chún正zhèng，为wéi人rén坦tǎn率shuài，言yán行xíng正zhèng派pài。

                                                  用法： 他tā是shì个gè光guāng明míng正zhèng大dà的de人rén，绝jué对duì不bú会huì做zuò出chū偷tōu鸡jī摸mō狗gǒu的de事shì。

                                                  Open and upright

                                                  How to use the metaphor: He is an open and upright person who will definitely stay away from any shady activity.
